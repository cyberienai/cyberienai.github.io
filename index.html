      Gemini Nano

      Our most efficient model for on-device tasks

      Build with Gemini

      1.0 Nano is optimized for providing quick responses, on-device — with or without a data network.

      1.0 Nano is optimized for providing quick responses, on-device — with or without a data network.

      Nano size, mega utility

      We're focused on making Nano the most powerful on-device model available. Later this year, Pixel and Android users will be the first to experience its new multimodal capabilities.

      Image understanding

      Richer and clearer descriptions of images and what’s in them.

      Speech transcription

      Understands what you’re saying so it’s possible to talk rather than type.

      Text summarization

      Distil messages, emails, and documents into concise, readable summaries.

      Demo

      Powerful, efficient understanding

      Nano transforms the on-device experience, enabling seamless interactions with low latency and privacy by design.

      Pixel Recorder

      The Pixel Recorder application uses Gemini Nano and AICore to power an on-device summarization feature.

      Gboard

      Gboard leverages Gemini Nano with AICore to provide accurate smart reply capabilities on-device.

      TalkBack

      Starting later this year, the TalkBack accessibility feature will use Nano’s multimodal capabilities to provide clear descriptions of images on Android phones.
